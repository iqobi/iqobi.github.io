Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Avatar is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app to create stylized avatars from your contact photos.

Data We Access

  • Contacts: We request access to your contacts to allow you to select which contact photos you want to transform.
  • Photos: We request access to your photo library to save generated avatars and access contact photos.

How We Use Your Data

  • Contact photos are temporarily uploaded to our secure servers only for the purpose of generating stylized avatars.
  • Generated avatars can be saved back to your contacts or photo library, based on your preferences.
  • We do not store your contacts or photos permanently on our servers. They are deleted after up to 48 hours.
  • All image processing is done through secure, encrypted connections.

Third-Party Services

  • revenuecat.com: Handles in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • dzine.ai: Processes photos to create stylized avatars.

Your Rights

  • You can revoke contact or photo access at any time through your device settings.
  • You can delete generated avatars from your contacts or photo library at any time.
  • You can request information about your data by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
